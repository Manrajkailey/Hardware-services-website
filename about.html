Our Story

Founded in 2013, TechFix Hardware Services began as a small repair shop with a simple mission: provide honest, high-quality hardware repair services at fair prices.

Over the past decade, we've grown into a trusted name in computer hardware repair, serving thousands of individual customers and businesses across the region. Our certified technicians have handled everything from simple screen replacements to complex enterprise networking installations.

Today, we continue to operate on the same core values that built our reputation: transparency, technical excellence, and genuine care for our customers' technology needs.
